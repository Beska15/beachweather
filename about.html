Discover Beach Weather's journey from Los Angeles, fusing catchy melodies with emotive lyrics. Experience our blend of alternative pop and indie rock, inviting you on an emotional journey. Join us as we share our passion for live music.

Our Story

Beach Weather began in 2015 in Los Angeles, California, when Nick Santino, Reeve Powers, and Austin Scates came together with a vision to create music that fused catchy melodies, emotive lyrics, and a summery vibe. Since then, we've been constantly evolving, exploring new sounds, and developing a unique style that sets us apart in the music scene.

Our Music

Our music is a blend of influences ranging from alternative pop to indie rock. We love experimenting with different sounds and crafting lyrics that tell our personal stories and reflect our deepest experiences. Each song is an opportunity to connect with our audience and share our passion for live music.

Our Commitment

At Beach Weather, we believe in and emotional connection in our music. We strive to create an environment where everyone feels welcome and understood, where our songs can serve as an escape from the outside world and an invitation to an emotional journey. We're excited to share our music with you and to continue creating memorable moments together.
